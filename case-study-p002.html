WhatsApp Conversational AI + Lead Nurture

A 3-agent WhatsApp system that qualifies, nurtures, and hands off leads — without a human in the loop.

5 Qualifying Questions Automated
3 AI Agents in Sequence
0 Cold Leads Reaching AMs

The Problem

A Singapore marketplace was generating around 100 Facebook Ads leads per day, but follow-up was entirely manual. Sales reps spent hours on WhatsApp handling cold replies, running qualifying questions, and answering FAQs.

Two compounding issues emerged: leads forgot they had signed up due to delayed response times, and the sheer volume meant genuine prospects went cold before an account manager could ever respond. The team was drowning in repetitive conversations while missing hot opportunities.

The Solution

A 3-workflow system anchored by a WhatsApp Trigger listener. The listener identifies each incoming lead from a live reference sheet and routes them through three AI agents in sequence. A parallel drip sequence workflow handles the post-signup nurture separately.

The system runs 24/7, ensuring every lead gets an instant, intelligent response — whether it's 2 PM or 2 AM. All conversation history is logged to Google Sheets in real time, giving AMs full context before their first human touchpoint.

AI Agent Architecture

AGENT 01

Lead Qualifier

Classifies incoming WhatsApp replies as Cold, Warm, or Ambiguous. Routes conversation to the appropriate next agent based on sentiment and intent analysis. Acts as the intelligent traffic cop for the entire system.

AGENT 02

Sales Assistant "Caro"

Runs 5 qualifying questions one at a time, conversationally. Reads full conversation history before every reply to avoid repetition. Never feels like a form or interrogation — maintains natural rapport while gathering critical qualification data.

AGENT 03

FAQ Agent

Keeps qualified leads warm post-qualification. Handles objections, surfaces relevant merchant success stories, and bridges to AM handoff at exactly the right moment. Ensures no lead goes cold while waiting for human follow-up.

Drip Nurture Sequence

Triggers on Google Sheets row change when a new lead arrives. The sequence runs independently from the conversational agents:

  • Day 0: Sends a personalized video welcome message immediately after signup
  • Day 2: Delivers social proof messages using real merchant success stories (Move Move Movers, Hackstage Builders, Yi Ki Door) matched to the lead's specific service category
  • Stage Tracking: Prevents duplicate sends and tracks which messages have been delivered

Key Prompt Engineering

"You are a friendly sales assistant. Your goal is to naturally guide the conversation toward completing 5 qualifying questions — one at a time, without making it feel like a form or interrogation. CONVERSATION HISTORY will be provided. Read it carefully to know what has already been discussed, which questions have been answered, and the current rapport with the lead."

This prompt ensures Caro maintains conversational flow while systematically gathering qualification data — never asking the same question twice, always building on previous rapport.
