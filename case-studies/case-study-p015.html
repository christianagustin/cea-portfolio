End-to-End Talent Acquisition Automation System

A six-system automation stack that transforms a manual, spreadsheet-based hiring process into a structured, trackable, and manager-visible talent acquisition pipeline.

The Problem

A manpower company with multiple Talent Acquisition officers, dozens of active client accounts, and hundreds of applicants in motion has a coordination problem. Each TA officer tracks their own applicants in their own sheet. No consistent format. No shared status view. Management cannot see what is happening without calling each TA individually.

The intake process compounds it. Leads from job fairs, referrals, walk-ins, and online forms arrive in different formats and get added to different sheets. There is no single source of truth for who is in the pipeline.

When a TA officer is out sick, their pipeline effectively pauses. When management wants a progress report, they make phone calls instead of opening a dashboard.

In a hiring business, your pipeline is your product. If you cannot see it clearly, you cannot deliver on it consistently.

How It Works

Six systems. Each one has a single job. Together they cover the entire applicant lifecycle from intake to onboarding handoff.

System 1: Lead Intake FormA standardized form replaces ad-hoc lead logging. Dedicated intake staff (not TA officers) fill out one form per applicant. Data writes directly to a master intake sheet with consistent column structure. Every lead enters through the same gate.
System 2: Round Robin DistributionWhen a new lead is submitted, the system assigns the applicant to one of seven TA officers following a strict rotation. The assignment position is stored and updated after every assignment. Management can see who owns each applicant at all times.
System 3: Auto-Generated Interview LinksWhen an applicant is ready for interview, the system generates a unique, pre-filled interview form link for that specific applicant. The link pre-fills the applicant's name and contact number. It is single-use — once submitted, permanently invalidated.
System 4: TA Sheet View and Status TrackingEach TA officer sees and edits only their own applicant rows and only the columns relevant to their role (A-Q). Operations columns (R onwards) are visible but locked. Status updates write back to the master record in real time.
System 5: Interview Form with OPS UnlockWhen the TA submits the interview form via their unique link, data writes to the applicant's master record. OPS-only columns are then unlocked for that row, allowing Operations to add onboarding, training, and endorsement data. Assessment outcomes follow defined terminology: Passed Hired, Passed-Pooling, Failed, Endorsed to Other Clients, No Answer.
System 6: Management DashboardA single view combining Executive Ops and Talent Acquisition dashboards. Shows: total applicants in pipeline, breakdown by TA officer, conversion rates at each stage, and client-specific hiring progress against targets. Updates automatically. No report to compile.

Role-Based Access Architecture

Intake StaffForm submission only. No direct sheet access.
TA OfficerColumns A-Q per applicant. Own rows only. Can update status, notes, and assessment outcomes.
OperationsColumns R onwards, unlocked only after interview form submission. Onboarding, training, and endorsement data.
ManagementFull dashboard. All pipeline data across all TAs and clients. Read-only aggregate view.

Outcome & Impact

  • Management has real-time pipeline visibility without calling or messaging any TA officer.
  • Every applicant is assigned fairly and automatically. No TA gets more than their share. No applicant sits unassigned.
  • Interview data is captured through a structured form. Assessment outcomes are consistent and reportable across all officers and clients.
  • The system scales. Adding an eighth TA officer means updating the rotation sequence. Everything else adapts automatically.
